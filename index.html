Numerical simulations for compressible multi-components flows involve many difficults.
On the one hand, high resolution schemes are needed to resolve delicate flow structures
such as Kelvin-Helmholtz instabilities and cell-like structures in supersonic flow.
On the another hand, most high resolution schemes may fail to capture sharp discontinuous
solutions robustly. Thus, high-fidelity solvers, which are accurate and robust, are highly demanded.
At the same time, the concept of high performance computing (HPC) will be implemented with GPU.
